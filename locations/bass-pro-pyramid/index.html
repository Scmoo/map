Locating

The Bass Pro Pyramid is a sporting goods store located off Interstate-40 at the west end of Memphis, Tennessee. It is a short 5 minute drive from the Arkansas and Tennessee border.

Photos

History

The Bass Pro Pyramid originated as a basketball arena in 1991, built by the city and county for ~$65 million in taxpayer dollars. At its completion, it was tha largest pyramid in America. 13 years later, the pyramid was abandoned as an arena in place of better basketball arenas. Bass Pro was given leasing rights to use the building and opened the "Bass Pro Shops at the Pyramid" in 2015.

The current Bass Pro Pyramid has two floors of merchandise that border 600,000 gallons of water. Live native fish and ducks can be found around the waterways. Several attractions include bowling, dining, and observation at the pyramid.

Important Notes

  • There is an indoor shooting range located on the second floor.
  • There is a bowling alley located near the back of the first floor.
  • Unconfirmed as of 2026, but some purchases in 2023 gave free elevator rides.
  • Two restaurants are located in the pyramid, one at the bottom and one at the top floor.
  • On site hotel lodging is available at the second floor of the pyramid.
  • The observation deck and top floor restaurant are accessible only by the elevator ride.
