Locating

Dr. Evermor's Sculpture Park is a collection of scrap metal reborn into a small village with the help of Tom Every. It is located off Highway 12 in the city of Sumpter, Wisconsin. The Foreverton stands in the center waiting to send humans "into the heavens" when activated.

Photos

Important Notes

  • The Foreverton's construction began in 1983 includes historical components including Thomas Edison's direct-current generators and a decontamination chamber from one of NASA's Apollo mission.
  • The artist, Tom Every, had collected scrap for WWII's effort and founded multiple salvage companies.
  • The park is 10 minutes nearby and on the way to a notable nature visit Devil's Lake, and is a roughly 40 minute drive to Madison, Wisconsin.
