Locating

Epic Systems is a healthcare IT company headquartered at the west end of Verona, Wisconsin nearby capital city Madison, Wisconsin. Public and visitor parking is available near the center of the Epic campus. Most buildings are completely wheelchair accessible and are connected with a mixture of underground tunnels and sky bridges for poor weather.
