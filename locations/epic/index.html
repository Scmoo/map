Locating

Epic Systems is a healthcare IT company headquartered at the west end of Verona, Wisconsin nearby capital city Madison, Wisconsin. Public and visitor parking is available near the center of the Epic campus. Most buildings are completely wheelchair accessible and are connected with a mixture of underground tunnels and sky bridges for poor weather.

Photos

History

The company Epic Systems was founded in 1979 by Judy Faulkner in Wisconsin. Epic originated as a master's degree project that focused on developing a way to track patient information electronically. After college, Judy got additional funding to turn that project into a healthcare company.

As of 2026, Epic has implemented software into hundreds of American hospital organizations and continues to branch internationally. Their U.S. campus continues to grow with buildings decorated after certain themes, such as Alice in Wonderland and a shipwrecked Castaway. Employees have an on-site culinary team to assist them in developing and implementing new hospitals with Epic.

Important Notes

  • The Mystery building has a hidden meeting room accessible through a disguised door.
  • The building "Fortress" has stairs leading to the roof with a dragon at the top.
  • North of the Farm Yard buildings is a small forest called Endor. At the north end of the bridge there is a treehouse that can be used for meetings.
  • One of Epic's internal mottos for the employees is "Do good. Have fun. Make money. In that order."
  • Before acquiring headquarters in Verona, Wisconsin, Epic held smaller staff meetings in movie theaters. This became tradition for all staff meetings to have popcorn.
  • Epic is a private company and prides themselves on being lead by developers and not business-oriented backgrounds. The future CEOs are limited to long-term Epic software seveloper employees only.
