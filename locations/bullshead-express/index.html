Locating

The BullsHead Express is a restaurant serving a blend of Japanese / American entrees. It's located off Old Bayshore Highway in Burlingame, California a 10 minute drive away from SFO airport.

Photos

History

Established at least since 2015 from the earliest review. History of the restaurant is limited.

Important Notes

  • You can order burgers and bento boxes. However, the best dishes are their specialty sushi rolls. There's nothing wrong with the other food but it would be a missed opportunity not to have the specialty rolls at this restaurant.
  • The restuarant at time of visiting seems to be solely owned and operated by an older couple. Both take extreme pride in their work.
